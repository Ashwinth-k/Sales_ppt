Key Decision Makers

Executive

Chairman / Principal

Focuses on institutional vision, accreditation (NAAC/NBA), and overall ROI.

"Will this improve our ranking and admissions?"

Operational

HODs / Coordinators

Manages day-to-day academic delivery, faculty workload, and student progress.

"Will this reduce my administrative headache?"

Technical

IT Admin

Responsible for software integration, data security, and platform scalability.

"Is it secure and easy to deploy?"
